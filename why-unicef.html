Why UNICEF?

We chose UNICEF because they are the world's most trusted organization for children.

🌎 Trusted, Global, Well-Known

UNICEF (the United Nations Children's Fund) has been working for children since 1946. They operate in over 190 countries and territories, making them the largest organization focused on children's rights and wellbeing in the world.

When you donate to UNICEF, you know your money is going to an organization with decades of experience, full transparency, and a proven track record of helping children get the education they deserve.

📚 What UNICEF Does for Education

UNICEF runs education programs in all five of the countries we focus on. Their work includes:

  • Building and repairing schools in underserved areas
  • Training teachers and improving the quality of education
  • Providing school supplies, textbooks, and learning materials
  • Running programs to get girls into classrooms
  • Supporting children affected by conflict and disaster to continue learning
  • Working with governments to improve education policy

💰 Where Your Money Goes

UNICEF is committed to using donations efficiently. Here's how your contribution makes an impact:

  • School supplies — pencils, notebooks, and backpacks for children who have none
  • Teacher training — helping educators deliver better lessons
  • School construction — building safe, accessible classrooms
  • Learning programs — after-school and catch-up programs for children who fell behind
  • Emergency education — keeping learning going during crises and disasters
