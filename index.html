Our Mission

What We Do

Educate The Children is a project dedicated to helping children across South Asia access the education they deserve. Across Bhutan, Bangladesh, India, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka, millions of kids are unable to attend school due to poverty, lack of infrastructure, and limited resources.

We partner with Room to Read, a charity focused entirely on children's literacy and girls' education across South Asia. Every dollar donated helps build libraries, publish books in local languages, and keep kids in school. Our goal is simple: every child should have the chance to learn.
